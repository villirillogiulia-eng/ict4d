Gambia Disaster Preparedness

Enhancing community resilience through data-driven risk assessment and health infrastructure monitoring in the North Bank Region.

The NBR Resilience Sentinel

The Civic Literacy Initiative (CLI) possesses deep regional expertise but faces a critical "granularity gap" where national-level data fails to inform community-level preparedness. Our proposal, the NBR Resilience Sentinel, bridges this gap by providing a state-of-the-art Geospatial and AI Dashboard designed to act as a "Mission Control Center" for CLI field teams.

Read Project Details

Precision Preparedness

Village-level monitoring allowing CLI to anticipate risks like droughts in the NBR with surgical accuracy.

Empowered Expertise

Validates local knowledge with real-time satellite evidence, acting as a "digital partner" for field staff.

Efficiency at Scale

Automated monitoring ensures CLI's work is efficient and easily scalable to other vulnerable regions.

Sustainable Impact

Capacity transfer through Train-the-Trainer (ToT) programs, making the system a permanent CLI asset.
